# Skill Gaps Tracker

Unmet needs discovered during work. When a gap reaches 3+ occurrences, it becomes
a candidate for `/evolve` to promote into a full skill.

**Format**:
```
### gap_name
- **Occurrences**: N
- **Dates**: YYYY-MM-DD, YYYY-MM-DD, ...
- **Context**: What was needed each time
- **Status**: tracking | candidate (3+) | promoted
```

---

*No gaps recorded yet. Gaps are added during `/reflect` when worklog entries have `skill: null`.*
